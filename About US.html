Get to Know Us

Kopi Mesin Cafe

Kopi Mesin is a popular café brand for coffee lovers in the East Coast of Malaysia, particularly in Terengganu and Kelantan. It combines the traditional art of coffee brewing with modern technology to deliver high-quality coffee experiences. Each Kopi Mesin outlet is strategically located and designed to provide a calm, comfortable, and welcoming environment for customers to relax, work, or socialize.

Why Choose a Kopi Mesin?

Premium Coffee

Only high-grade coffee beans are selected to ensure consistent aroma and taste.

Aesthetic Atmosphere

Minimalist interior design, perfect for photography enthusiasts.

Miscellaneous Menu

Not just coffee, we provide Western and local dishes that cater to everyone's tastes.

Digitally Friendly

Equipped with high-speed Wi-Fi and comfortable spaces for students and workers.

Our Vision

To be the preferred café chain in Kelantan and Terengganu, offering a unique coffee experience that blends tradition with modern lifestyle.

Our Mission

Providing high-quality coffee, cozy and aesthetically pleasing café spaces, and a welcoming environment that caters to students, workers, and coffee enthusiasts in Kelantan and Terengganu.
